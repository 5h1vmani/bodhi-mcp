# LinkedIn Company Page Strategy

> Decision framework for company page setup, content distribution, and the employee advocacy model.

## TL;DR

- **Company pages get 1.6% organic reach** - personal profiles get 561% more; use both, not either/or
- **First 200 characters are the entire pitch on mobile** - front-load proof points before the fold
- **Employee advocacy is the distribution model** - company page = credibility anchor, personal profiles = reach engine
- **Saves/Sends now outweigh likes** - create reference-worthy content people forward to colleagues
- **Products tab is underused** - showcase individual products with their own follower base and CTAs

## Decision Guide

| Scenario                                | Approach                                                        | Why                                                               |
| --------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Founding team < 10 people               | Founder profile as primary channel, company page as credibility | Small teams get more reach through personal posts than page posts |
| Launching company page from scratch     | Complete all profile fields before first post                   | LinkedIn ranks complete pages higher in search results            |
| Writing the About section               | Front-load first 200 chars with specifics and proof points      | Mobile truncates after ~200 chars; most visitors never expand     |
| Choosing content for company page       | Product updates, milestones, hiring; not thought leadership     | Thought leadership performs better from personal profiles         |
| Maximizing post reach                   | Post from founder profile, company page reshares                | Personal posts get 561% more reach than company page posts        |
| Building SEO visibility on LinkedIn     | Use all 20 specialty slots with keyword-rich terms              | Specialties are indexed by LinkedIn search                        |
| Showcasing a specific product           | Use Products tab with dedicated page, CTA, and media            | Products tab builds its own follower base separate from page      |
| Driving credibility for sales team      | Keep company page polished: banner, About, featured, followers  | Prospects check company page after seeing employee posts          |
| Scaling beyond founder-led distribution | Employee advocacy program: equip team with post templates       | 10 employees sharing > 1 company page posting                     |

## Content Distribution Model

| Channel           | Role                  | Content Type                        | Expected Reach    |
| ----------------- | --------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| Founder profile   | Primary reach engine  | Thought leadership, stories, takes  | 5-15% of network  |
| Employee profiles | Amplification layer   | Reshares + personal angle           | 3-8% each         |
| Company page      | Credibility anchor    | Product updates, milestones, hiring | 1-2% of followers |
| Company page      | Reshare from profiles | Amplify founder/employee posts      | Minimal added     |

**The flow:** Founder posts original content. Employees reshare with personal context. Company page publishes product/milestone content and occasionally reshares high-performing personal posts.

## Company Page Setup

| Element                 | Requirement                                                | Common Error                            |
| ----------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| About (first 200 chars) | Specific proof points: what you do, scale, differentiation | Generic mission statement               |
| About (full)            | Problem, approach, proof, CTA. Under 2,000 chars           | Copying website copy verbatim           |
| Tagline                 | Keep the brand tagline for cross-channel consistency       | Writing a different tagline per channel |
| Specialties             | All 20 slots, keyword-rich, no redundancy                  | Leaving slots empty                     |
| Banner                  | Product screenshot or data visualization, not stock photo  | Generic stock imagery                   |
| CTA button              | "Visit website" or "Contact us" based on goal              | Default "Follow" only                   |
| Products tab            | One entry per product with media, description, CTA         | Not using Products tab at all           |
| Featured                | Pin 2-3 best-performing posts or key announcements         | Leaving Featured empty                  |

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake                                            | Fix                                                                          |
| -------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Posting thought leadership from company page       | Post from founder/employee profiles; company page for product and milestones |
| Generic About section ("We are passionate about")  | Front-load first 200 chars with specifics: product, scale, differentiation   |
| Ignoring the Products tab                          | Set up Products with media, descriptions, and dedicated CTAs                 |
| Same content on company page and founder profile   | Different content types: page = product news, founder = insight and stories  |
| Chasing company page followers over employee reach | 10 employees with 500 connections each = 5,000 reach vs 1,000 page followers |
| Writing a different tagline for LinkedIn           | Use brand tagline consistently across all channels                           |
| Posting without completing profile setup           | Fill all fields (About, banner, specialties, CTA) before first post          |

## Checklist

Company page setup:

- [ ] About section front-loads proof points in first 200 characters
- [ ] All 20 specialty slots filled with keyword-rich terms
- [ ] Custom banner (product screenshot or data visualization, not stock)
- [ ] CTA button set to appropriate action (Visit website / Contact us)
- [ ] Products tab configured with media and descriptions
- [ ] 2-3 posts pinned to Featured section
- [ ] Tagline matches brand tagline

Distribution model:

- [ ] Founder profile is primary thought leadership channel
- [ ] Company page content is product updates, milestones, hiring
- [ ] Employee reshare workflow established (templates or guidelines)
- [ ] Content is not duplicated identically across page and profiles
